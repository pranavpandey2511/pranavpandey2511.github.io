I build AI systems where the interesting work starts after the first demo. The problems I enjoy most sit at the boundary of model behavior, retrieval quality, evaluation, infrastructure, and product design.

Today I am a Senior Applied AI Engineer at SpotDraft, where I work on the platform and application layer behind products such as VerifAI and Sidebar. That work includes agent orchestration with LangGraph, retrieval pipelines over contracts and policies, multi-model routing with LiteLLM, prompt design, evaluation patterns, and the surrounding engineering needed to make AI useful inside legal workflows.

Before SpotDraft, I helped build Apollo.io's AI assistant and ranking systems, co-founded Stybe and led the AI search and personalization stack, and shipped perception systems at Motive, XTRA, and Wipro. Earlier projects spanned facial recognition, multi-task computer vision, real-time pose estimation, one-shot detection, document denoising, and explainable healthcare ML.

## Where I add the most value

- Turning fuzzy product ideas into reliable AI systems with clear architecture and quality loops.
- Building agentic workflows that combine orchestration, retrieval, tooling, and fallbacks instead of relying on a single prompt.
- Designing evaluation harnesses that make regressions visible and product quality measurable.
- Owning systems end to end, from modeling and retrieval to APIs, infrastructure, rollout, and iteration with users.

## Research roots

My foundation is in applied machine learning and computer vision, especially in settings where data is noisy, coverage is incomplete, or deployment conditions are messy. I have published work on pedestrian interaction and safety for autonomous vehicles, and I still bring that research mindset into product work today: understand failure modes, design the right feedback loop, and make the system measurable.
