What I build

Systems where model intelligence is only one piece of the job.

The strongest AI products come from the combination of retrieval quality, orchestration strategy, evaluation discipline, infrastructure judgment, and close contact with the real workflow. That is the layer I enjoy most.

Agent Orchestration

Designing multi-step and multi-agent workflows that can reason, call tools, recover from failure modes, and stay useful inside real product constraints.

Retrieval and Knowledge Systems

Building hybrid search, vector retrieval, semantic matching, and knowledge-backed reasoning systems for domains where context quality matters more than model bravado.

Evaluation Harnesses

Creating golden datasets, prompt and policy checks, regression loops, and quality metrics that let teams improve AI behavior intentionally instead of by anecdote.

Productizing AI

Moving from research or prototype energy to production leverage by owning the plumbing around latency, cost, reliability, observability, and user trust.

Selected work

A career shaped by platform thinking, shipped systems, and cross-domain AI depth.

My background spans legal tech, sales automation, AI-native commerce, perception, computer vision, and applied research. Across each environment, the work has been about building systems that survive contact with production.

SpotDraft 2025 to present

Senior Applied AI Engineer

Architected and built GenAI platform infrastructure serving products such as VerifAI and Sidebar. The platform powers contract review, compliance checks, legal research, and agentic assistance for legal teams working inside high-context workflows.

I built orchestration flows with LangGraph, retrieval pipelines over contracts and policies, multi-model routing with LiteLLM, and evaluation patterns that kept responses consistent across legal use cases while helping the product scale quickly.

Apollo.io 2025

Senior Machine Learning Engineer

Core contributor on the AI assistant team, where I helped build chat-native workflows for lead generation, list creation, and email sequencing. I evaluated supervisor, mesh, and ReAct-style agent patterns and built a golden-dataset-based harness that reached 87% coverage.

I also rebuilt a URL prioritizer for large-scale company website scraping, expanding coverage from 35% to 75%+ and removing roughly $7,000 per month in spend through custom models.

Stybe 2023 to 2025

Co-Founder and CTO

Built the AI-native core of a constantly learning fashion commerce product, including conversational discovery, hybrid search, product matching, and feedback-driven personalization.

The platform blended sparse and dense retrieval, LLM-assisted tagging, semantic similarity, and user feedback loops to make fashion discovery feel closer to working with a personal stylist than a search box.

Motive and XTRA 2021 to 2023

Production ML Across Vision and Perception

Shipped facial recognition, multi-task perception models, and real-time pose-based systems across both edge and cloud contexts, including 100K+ daily predictions and deployments that reached more than one million devices.

Earlier work included activity recognition, browser-side scoring systems, and fitness recommendation products built from video understanding pipelines.

Wipro and research 2020 to 2021

Applied Research Foundations

Worked on document denoising with CycleGANs, one-shot symbol recognition, incremental learning, audio-based cough detection, and an explainable COVID detection app. Published work on pedestrian interaction and safety for autonomous vehicles.
